**See what's actually happening in your business. In 10 seconds.**

T4H Widget Dock loads your business intelligence widgets in a Chrome side panel — no switching tabs, no waiting for reports, no guessing.

Connect your T4H account and instantly see:

- AI Ops Dashboard — full lifecycle for every AI agent run
- System Waste Detector — unused tables, dead lambdas, gaps

**Finance & Compliance**

**Growth**

- AI Offer Validator — score ideas and campaigns before you build
- AI Sales Assistant — lead tracking and follow-up automation

**Platform**
- Universal Widget Engine — render any widget from your Supabase snippets
- AI Command Terminal — fire Lambda functions from your browser
- Enterprise Asset Register — all IP, classified and RDTI-tagged

---

### How it works

1. Install the extension
2. Open Settings → paste your Supabase anon key
3. Click the extension icon → Open Side Panel
4. Your entitled widgets load automatically

Every widget shows a **REAL / PARTIAL / PRETEND** badge — so you always know what's production-verified vs in progress.

### Privacy

This extension connects only to your own Supabase instance using the API key you provide. No data is sent to T4H servers. Your business data stays in your infrastructure.

Full privacy policy: tech4humanity.com.au/privacy

---

### Permissions explained

- **storage** — saves your API key locally in Chrome
- **sidePanel** — opens the widget dock beside your browser
- **activeTab** — reads current tab URL for context
- **notifications** — alerts for stuck runs or failures

Host permissions are limited to your Supabase instance and T4H product domains.
